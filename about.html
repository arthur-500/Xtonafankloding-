About XTONAFAN KLODIN

Redefining luxury bespoke tailoring and ready-to-wear fashion from Effutu, Central Region, Ghana.

The Philosophy

Crafting Excellence with Every Stitch

Founded with a passion for meticulous craftsmanship and exceptional design, XTONAFAN KLODIN brings a fresh perspective to luxury apparel. Located at Liberia Camp GPRTU in Effutu, Central Region, our studio serves discerning clients who appreciate premium fabrics, flawless fits, and timeless elegance.

Whether crafting custom bespoke suits for men, elegant dresses for women, or charming attire for boys and girls, our master tailors combine traditional tailoring artistry with contemporary style.
